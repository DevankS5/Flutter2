## Usage Notes

- All images must be **web-optimized** (compress JPEGs to ~85% quality, PNGs with max optimization)
- Place all files under `c:\Users\devan\Downloads\AJW\public\images\`
- Logo PNGs must have **transparent backgrounds**
- Avatar photos will be displayed in **circular crop** — center the face in the frame
- Device mockup screens (files 3–7) are used inside CSS-rendered phone frames — provide only the **screen content, no device hardware**
